HIV is a virus that attacks the immune system by killing white blood cells. Since white blood cells help fight infections, without an abundance of them you're more suspectible to dying from just a common cold. Once your white blood cell count is below 200, you're now AIDS positive. The difference between HIV and AIDS is that HIV may not be curable but treatable.
